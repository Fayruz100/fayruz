Introduction to CyberSecurity!

Hi welcome to my HTML website, I will be sharing about why its important to PROTECT your PERSONAL DATA ONLINE.

And the CONSEQUENCES behind not protecting it.

What is personal data?

  • Your Full Government name
  • Your NRIC Number
  • Your DOB

These are your personal data that are IMPORTANT TO YOU, anyone who gets a hold of your personal data might COMMIT IDENTITY FRAUD and CARRY OUT SCAMS by using your identity to fool your loved ones.

Why must we protect our personal data online?

  • Prevents Identity Theft – Stops criminals from stealing your identity and committing fraud.
  • Avoids Scams & Hacking – Reduces risks of phishing, scams, and cyberattacks.
  • Protects Privacy – Keeps your sensitive information (photos, messages, finances) private.
  • Stops Unwanted Tracking – Limits companies from monitoring and targeting you with ads.
  • Saves Money & Reputation – Prevents financial loss and damage to your reputation.

What is Personal Data?

  • Basic Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, home address, and date of birth.
  • Sensitive Information: Racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, health data, and sexual orientation.
  • Digital Identifiers: IP addresses, cookie IDs, and online account information.
  • Physical Characteristics: Photographs, audio recordings, and video footage of oneself

    • These are your personal data that are IMPORTANT TO YOU, anyone who gets a hold of your personal data might COMMIT IDENTITY FRAUD and CARRY OUT SCAMS by using your identity to fool your loved ones.

    Why Must We Protect Our Personal Data Online?
      1. Prevents Identity Theft – Stops criminals from stealing your identity and committing fraud.
      2. Avoids Scams & Hacking – Reduces risks of phishing, scams, and cyberattacks.
      3. Protects Privacy – Keeps your sensitive information (photos, messages, finances) private.
      4. Stops Unwanted Tracking – Limits companies from monitoring and targeting you with ads.
      5. Saves Money & Reputation – Prevents financial loss and damage to your reputation.
